## 1. Renewables / Solar Solutions
**Overview:** Comprehensive solar energy and load-shedding mitigation for the South African residential and commercial sectors.

### Product Range
*   **Solar Panels:** High-efficiency Monocrystalline and Bifacial modules.
    *   **Brands:** Canadian Solar (TopHiKu series), JA Solar (N-Type Bifacial), Jinko Solar (Tiger Neo).
    *   **Key Specs:** 460W to 620W+ output; 25-year linear power warranty.
*   **Inverters:** Intelligent hybrid systems managing Eskom grid, solar, and battery power.
    *   **Brands:** Sunsynk (5kW, 8kW, 12kW, 16kW), Deye, Victron Energy (MultiPlus-II).
    *   **Features:** Remote monitoring via Wi-Fi/GPRS, parallel capability (up to 16 units for large commercial installs), IP65 waterproof rating.
*   **Energy Storage (Batteries):** Lithium Iron Phosphate (LiFePO4) deep-cycle batteries.
    *   **Brands:** Freedom Won, Sunsynk, Pylontech, Dyness.
    *   **Lifespan:** 6,000+ cycles (approx. 10–15 years).

### Technical & Market Context (South Africa)
*   **Load-Shedding Protection:** Systems designed for seamless switchover (<20ms) to ensure no downtime for computers or appliances.
*   **NERSA & Municipal Compliance:** All inverters installed are on the City of Cape Town / City Power approved list for SSEG (Small Scale Embedded Generation).
*   **Tax Incentives (Section 12B):** Businesses can claim back up to 125% of the solar asset value as a tax deduction in the first year (check latest SARS updates).

### Pricing Range (Estimates)
*   **5kW Entry-Level (Load-Shedding Backup):** R65,000 – R85,000
*   **8kW Hybrid (Full Residential Solar):** R120,000 – R160,000
*   **Commercial (50kW+):** Custom quote based on R12,000 – R15,000 per kWp installed.

## 2. Telecommunications & Business Voice
**Overview:** Modern VoIP (Voice over IP) and high-speed data connectivity to replace legacy copper lines.

### Solutions
*   **Hosted PBX:** Cloud-based phone system. No hardware maintenance; managed via web portal.
*   **VoIP Hardware:** High-quality desk phones and conferencing units.
    *   **Brands:** Yealink (T3, T4, T5 series), Fanvil (X series), Grandstream.
*   **Connectivity:**
    *   **Business Fibre (FTTB):** Low contention, synchronous speeds (e.g., 50/50Mbps to 1Gbps).
    *   **LTE/5G Failover:** Automatic switch to wireless data if fibre is severed.

### Key Features
*   **Capping & Billing:** Per-second billing with significantly lower rates than traditional Telkom landlines.
*   **Remote Work:** Use the PBX mobile app to take office calls anywhere in the world.
*   **Number Porting:** Keep existing 011, 012, 021, etc., geographic numbers.

### FAQ / Selling Points
*   **"Will it work during load-shedding?"** Yes, if the router and phones are connected to an EcoIP Solar/UPS system.
*   **"Is the voice quality good?"** We use dedicated Voice VLANs to prioritize call traffic over web browsing.

## 3. Printers & Managed Print Services (MPS)
**Overview:** Optimized document management to reduce the "cost-per-page" for South African offices.

### Product Range
*   **Brands:** HP, Kyocera, Brother, Epson.
*   **Models:** Desktop MFPs (Medium) to high-volume A3 floor-standing copiers.
*   **Consumables:** Toner-based (Lasers) and high-capacity Ink-Tank (EcoTank/PageWide) systems.

### Managed Print Model
*   **Cost-per-Page (CPP):** Businesses pay only for what they print. Toner, parts, and service are included in the click rate.
*   **Auto-Toner Replenishment:** Software alerts EcoIP to ship toner before the client runs out.
*   **Leasing Options:** 36 to 60-month rental agreements via EcoIP Finance (see Section 8).

### Competitive Advantage
*   Local 4-hour on-site response time for hardware repairs.
*   Detailed monthly reporting on who is printing what to prevent waste.
