Common Questions

Will it work during load-shedding?

Yes, provided your router and phones are powered by a UPS or one of our Solar solutions. The cloud PBX itself is always online.

Is the voice quality good?

Absolutely. We implement dedicated Voice VLANs to prioritize your call traffic over general internet browsing, ensuring crystal clear quality.
